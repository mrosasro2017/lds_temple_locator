LDS Temples


The LDS Temples are special places for LDS members because they are places dedicated to worship the Lord. They are places built with the most precious materials and maintained in that pristine state every day. LDS People believe that in the temples they can perform ordinances for their already deceased relatives. That is one of the reasons why LDS people are so centered in families and in doing family history work. Personally I would love to be able to visit every temple in the globe someday. For that reason I am always looking opportunities to find temples in the places I visit.

For that reason a way to find temples by location(by state/ by country) with specific details should be really useful.

Instructions

Country Locator

  • Click the "Select Country" dropdown and select the country of interest. The map will display the country's temples.

State Locator

  • Click the "Select State" dropdown and select the desired state in the USA. The map will display the state's temples.

Click on individual temple

  • When clicked, each station will display pictures and temple metadata.
